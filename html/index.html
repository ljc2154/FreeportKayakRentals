About Freeport Kayak Rentals

Mission Statement

Freeport Kayak Rentals' goal is to provide its customers with the ability to experience the natural beauty that the local bays have to offer. The launch location at Waterfront Park provides the perfect opportunity to paddle through the waters of Baldwin Bay and Middle Bay. With 16 Kayaks and SUPs (Stand-up Paddleboards) on hand, FKR offers lessons or rentals to groups of various sizes.

About our Owner

Freeport Kayak Rentals is owned and operated by Jerry Foster. Jerry has lived on Long Island his entire life and currently lives in Merrick with his wife and three children. Outside of running FKR, Jerry is very involved with Operation Splash, a volunteer organization committed to improving the quality of life on Long Island shores.
