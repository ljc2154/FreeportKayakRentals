Reservations

Spend a few hours or the whole day paddling our beautiful calm bays, catching some rays, and discovering the natural beauty of our local waterways. Make a reservation and we will meet you at Waterfront Park with your Kayaks ready to go!

Hours of operation

8am-4pm Thursdays, Fridays, Saturdays, Sundays, and Holidays.

Operating Memorial Day Weekend through Labor Day Weekend.

Rates

  • Single Kayaks: $40 for 2 hours, $60 for the whole day
  • Double Kayaks: $60 for 2 hours, $80 for the whole day
  • Stand-up Paddleboards: $40 for 2 hours, $60 for the whole day
    •

Prices include sales tax as well as kayak, paddle, and life jacket rentals.
