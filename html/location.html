Location

We launch at Waterfront Park and venture West into Baldwin Bay and even into Middle Bay. Stop off at "Wampum Island" and collect a few purple-streaked sea shells for a special keepsake of the day. Paddle the creeks due South of Waterfront Park and be amazed at the peaceful beauty that surrounds you. Make your way around the big islands for a great workout!

Getting here

Waterfront Park is located at S Long Beach Ave in Freeport, NY. For directions from wherever you may be, click the blue position marker on the map and then click directions in the bottom left. Zoom in and out on the map to get a better feel for our location.

Local Attractions

The Nautical Mile is home to some of the finest restaurants and shops in the area. After you paddle, be sure to stop by for a tasty ice cream cone, a refreshing cold beverage, or a delicious dinner.
