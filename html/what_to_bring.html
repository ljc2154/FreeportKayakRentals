Tips

Here are a few tips to help you enjoy your day on the bay even more!

What to bring to the bay

  • Bathing suit
  • Sunscreen
  • Hat
  • Cheap sunglasses
  • Change of clothes
  • Old sneakers or water shoes
  • Ziplock bags for clothing
  • Insect repellent
  • Disposable waterproof camera
  • Money to spend at the Nautical Mile
  • Someone to paddle with!
    •

What NOT to bring (sorry, it's the rules)

  • Anyone under the age of 7 (Not allowed on kayaks)
  • Anything you don't want to get wet, sandy, damaged, destroyed, or lost
  • Alcohol
    •

What we already have at Waterfront Park

  • Bathrooms
  • Washdown spigot
  • Gazebo
  • Picnic tables and bar-b-cue grills (first-come, first-serve)
